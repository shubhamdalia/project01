About Us

Welcome to
EduGene Institute

EduGene Institute has been formed with an AIM to provide “EDUCATION FOR EVERY STUDENT”.

We are a group of people who are passionate in every aspect of the education sector. Our team is derived from people who have experience of teaching in the best of the educational institutes in India.

The Founder of EduGene Institute has a very good background from education sector, she taught students since 2008 at multiple coaching and college of India(Bhilai, Varanasi, Patiala) for board exams and competitive exams as well.

We all know, up to class 12 education is base of everything, either you want to be an engineer, doctor, pilot, scientist, entrepreneur or an IAS officer. India is a vast country where many students are not able to get good education support due to many reasons such as remote areas, unavailability of educational infrastructure, huge travelling time in metro cities, insufficiency of funds and many more. We are trying to reach to every student throughout country to provide them best support for their after-school education by multiple ways.

We are operating Offline Tuition/Coaching Centers for “Class 5 - 12 (CBSE,ICSE and Punjab Board students)” at near Focal point and #78 CHINAR BAGH and planned to have new centers soon, where we deliver classes with collaboration of very experienced faculties to small group of students.

We believe into student’s results. We don’t compromise with the standard of education and we do follow a process to make it perfect. Our many students get more than 90% marks in their Board Exams.
